About

Aadya Talreja is an aspiring computer science engineer currently pursuing her BTech (Honours) degree at RVU, Bangalore. As an absolute workaholic, no task proves to be too much and any task will always be met with total passion and dedication. She dreams of being employed in the field of Cyber Security and isn't afraid to dream big while also making sure to dream smart.

Aadya is a born leader and comes equipped with all the social skills one would look for in a person. Her relentless passion towards coding resulted in a full circle moment with her going back to judge Transcendence 2022 a fest that she once passionately organised as a high school student. Aadya is a bunch of jokes and laughter but can also quickly adapt to situations when needed, she really is the whole package!
